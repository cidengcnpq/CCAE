To use the image data, you must extract the .zip files first. Files from parts 1 and 2 must be merged before testing the code.

d_0_intact.zip
Intact (training set): Normal condition used for training and validation, representing 70% of undamaged data.

d_0_unknown.zip
Intact (unknown): Normal condition used for testing, representing 30% of undamaged data.

d_1 - part1.zip + d_1 - part2.zip
Damage 1: Loosened bolts on all floors at both ends of the beam on the east face, north side.

d_2 - part1.zip + d_2 - part2.zip
Damage 2: Loosened bolts on floors 1 and 2 at both ends of the beam on the east face, north side.
